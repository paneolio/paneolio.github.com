Delicatezze Toscane || Catering || Trattoria

A proposal of typical Tuscan dishes prepared by following the ancient popular recipes


Lasagna alla Bolognese

We follow the long-established classic Lasagna recipe, preparing the layers with italian flour, using selected mixes of beef and pork meat and imported italian cheese.

Lasagna agli Asparagi

Fresh asparagus cooked following the traditional procedure - combined with red zafran and selected italian cheeses make this recipe very tasty and peculiar.

Rustico

A fast and easy warm snack which wraps the wild taste of southern italian fresh tomato, oregano and capers.

Timballo di Pasta

A mix of pasta, cheese and meat cooked in the oven, a very widespread italian main course

Cornetti al Cioccolato

The original homemade Cornetti al Cioccolato, the flag of the italian breakfast; around 10 a.m. we normally serve them straight from the oven!

Other Recipes

  • Focaccine

    A rich selection of homemade focaccia, filled with fine italian delicacies, such as provolone, mozzarella, dry tomato, rucola and different Tuscan hams, always dressed with Tuscan olive oil and fresh southern oregano

  • Pizza

    Sliced pizza from homemade dough using organic flour and fresh ingredients, mostly imported directly from Italy; the slice is a very practical format if you want to have a quick and cheap meal and enjoy the most original pizza recipe

  • Peperoni Ripieni

    Paprika stuffed with provolone cheese, mozzarella, basil and onions; real authentic dish from the south of Italy


The Team

  • Pane (aka Fabio)

    My name is Fabio and I welcome you in our small and humble place, where we try to preserve the good italian food traditions and share it in a welcoming place like the center of Amsterdam is. In my family there's a long trail of chiefs, all sharing the same love for simple, healthy and tasty food, mixing different origins and cultures. Pane & Olio is also the result of this journey, mixed with the flavor of the Tuscan products that we directly import from there, working with friends and relatives that keep this project alive.


  • Olio (aka Paolo)

    Hey there, welcome to our website! My name is Paolo and I'm normally hidden behind the kitchen, preparing the dough, cooking the pasta and dressing the dishes of the picture! I'm a passionate chief coming from the farest tip of Puglia, a region full of excellent products and simple recipes, yet extremely tasty. My daily satisfaction is to share my experience with people who enjoy our food and our mission.


Friends


A Little Company Information

Pane & Olio has opened in July 2010, always based on the effort of these two brave guys, Fabio and Paolo; the main objective of this place is to be a nice, quiet and relaxing place for visitors and local where they can enjoy some real and traditional italian food, the way our grandmas taught us. It is also an attempt to create a welcoming environment for everyone and enjoy the company of people around a table, the way our fathers taught us. The location of Pane & Olio is indeed not a coincidence; we're located in the heart of one the most beautiful, liberal and welcoming city in the world; thanks to the warmth collaboration of friends, colleagues, mates and even customers we feel already part of it and we work everyday to make our customers feel special.
